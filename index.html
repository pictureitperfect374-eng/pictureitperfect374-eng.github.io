Frequently asked questions

Helpful information before you request an estimate

Don't see your question? Use the form below and include it in the project details.

Are estimates free?

Yes. You can submit the online estimate request form at no cost.

How will I be contacted?

Until a business phone number is added, Picture It Perfect will follow up by email.

Do I need to move furniture before interior painting?

Project preparation depends on the room and scope. Include furniture details in your estimate request so preparation can be discussed ahead of time.

How long will my project take?

Timing depends on the size of the project, surface condition, preparation needs, weather for outdoor work, and scheduling. A clearer estimate can be provided after the project is reviewed.

Can I request more than one service?

Yes. Select the main service in the form and list any additional work in the project-details box.
